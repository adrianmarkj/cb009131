LOLA THE LEOPARD

Leopards, including lions, tigers, and jaguars, are graceful and dominant big cats. Sub-Saharan Africa, northeast Africa, Central Asia, India, and China are where they live.

LOLA'S BACKSTORY

Lola was rescued from an impoverished family in Yala that had taken her in as a cub; not knowing that she was in fact a leopard and not a kitten until she had grown a mnonth or two. Once the family had noticed their predicament Lola was placed in the care of Animal Control and was bound to be released to the Yala National Park, yet seeing as though she was more docile and seemed to prefer the company of humans as opposed to her own species she was taken in by the Dehiwala Zoo.

MEET LOLA

Meeting a carnivorous animal for the first time can be daunting, especially when it's one as big and infamous as a jungle cat, but fear not! Lola is an extremely gentle and docile big cat! As long as you don't show aggressive behaviour towards her or excite her you will be fine. And of course our trained keeper Elizabeth will be with you every step of the way.

MEET ELIZABETH

Elizabeth is our most senior and experienced leopard keeper. She has over 10 years of experience as a professional keeper and has been around animals all of her life due to being raised in her grandparents' safari. There are no safer hands to be in when face-to-face with the beast itself.
